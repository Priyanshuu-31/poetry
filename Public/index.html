On Writing

as I always say, poetry begins where logic ends.

sometimes, i write because it hurts. sometimes, because it heals. but mostly, i write because some feelings don't have an emoji yet.

these lines aren't always polished. they're not meant to be. they're raw, honest, and mostly scribbled at 2am with a tired soul and a hopeful heart.

Poetry

Here goes my first poetry:

In hush of night, the moonlight spills,
Like silver ink on window sills.
The stars hum low, the wind walks light,
And dreams take flight in velvet night.
No need for maps, no need for signs,
The heart remembers secret lines.
So close your eyes and let it roam,
Each star above will guide you home.
